About Dr.Kedir Monjor

General Manager of Assela Rehoboth Hospital and Medical College

Dr. Kedir Monjor, born in Arsi Zone, Kofele district, accomplished his primary and secondary schooling in 1963 E.C. at Kofele and Asella towns; and then joined Addis Ababa University Medical College in Gondar where he graduated with the Degree of Doctor of Medicine in 1981E.C After having served for twelve years as a General practitioner at Abomsa Health Center and Asella Hospital, he went back to college and specialized in the field of pediatrics in 1997E.C. As a result of his strong commitment to serve his country. Dr. Kedir opened a clinic and a medical college at Diploma level which he up-graded the clinic to the status of a General Teaching Hospital and promoted the college to Degree level, established a whole-sale center (shop) for Drugs and Medical equipment, and an Academy from grades 1-12 in Asella town with a total investment of Eth. Birr 155,000,000 (one hundred and fifty five million) over the last thirty years. More importantly, Dr. Kedir has opened job opportunity to nearly 300 employees with different levels of education in Health-Sciences and other fields of study who are now working in the four sister companies. In general, Dr. Kedir has relentlessly continued serving the community and fellow citizens in the area of Education and Training and provision of Quality Health-care at an affordable service cost for all customers irrespective of Gender, age, color, ethnicity, Language and religion differences.
